About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Mama Chang‘s open for business in 2006. We started serving Chinese dishes such as fried rice, chicken lo mein, sesame, chicken, egg-rolls, etc. In 2016 we started serving Japanese food, such as Yakisoba, Udon, Ramen, and takoyaki. Recently in 2022 we started serving Korean food such as Mondo and Korean corn dog. We are planning to expand into desserts, such as mochi doughnut.

When we first opened the food truck, it was more of a hobby, then ate business. Mama Chang enjoyed cooking and meeting new people as well as driving to new places and exploring different towns. Now that mama Chang has expanded We have plans to upgrade our fleet as well as explore new events. Mama Chang still enjoys going out and meeting people as well as catching up with old friends.

Mama Chang also does catering for weddings, birthday parties, graduation parties, and businesses. We would love to get in touch for any inquiries or questions.
